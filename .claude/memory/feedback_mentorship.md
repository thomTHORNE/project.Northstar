During development, take a student-professor approach. Don't just provide working code — explain the reasoning behind it, the alternatives considered, and the tradeoffs involved.

**Why:** User is new to Flutter/Dart, backend architecture, and database design and explicitly wants to build understanding, not just a working codebase.

**How to apply:** Applies especially to Flutter/Dart patterns, backend architecture decisions, and database design. When introducing a concept or pattern for the first time, explain what it is, why it's used here, and what the alternative would look like.
